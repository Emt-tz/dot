Introduction

A digital finance match-making platform facilitating peer to business lending marketplace for investors to lend money directly to small and medium-sized businesses and micro-entrepreneurs. Circle Financing Portal takes the advantage of operating online to provide the services more cheaply than traditional financial institutions.

Mission

The Challenge being addressed

According to Financial Inclusion Insights in Tanzania, 6 in 10 adults (56% of the current population) are financially included, mostly through mobile money accounts. Tanzania Chamber of Commerce, Industry and Agriculture says 95% of all businesses in Tanzania are SMEs which contribute to 35% to the annual Gross Domestic Product (GDP) in our country; and most of these lack access to financing.


Who is the solution for? Describe your customer

CFP works for small and medium-sized enterprises and micro-entrepreneurs in need of a wide spectrum of financing services and products who fail to access such services from traditional financial institutions.
