Vision

We envision a world with a confident women, where she can find help on mental issues while embracing her natural body and physical changes

Mission

Creating a place where women can truly be themselves


The Challenge being addressed

Women struggle to prioritize themselves and many of them struggle with mental issues caused by abuse (In Tanzania almost 2 in 5 women aged 15 to 49 years experienced physical violence at some point in their life), family and relationship problems, lack of body acceptance and they don’t have a right person to talk to. Furthermore, women don’t get the right information on mental issues and lack of space where they can safely and freely talk and discuss their mental struggles.
Primary the clinic will be in Kilimanjaro then other region in Tanzania, which currently will reach them through event, social media campaign, radio and Tv programs.


Who is the solution for? Describe your customer

The solution is for women aged 20-45 who experience abuse/violence in her life, un/married women, un/employed women suffering inside, a woman who don’t have a right person to talk to. A woman passes through hardship in life and she is not comfortable with her body


What support do you need to bring the innovation to life

The support I need to launch my solution is a physical place (Venue) legal and regulation help, partnership with other organisation which deals with women going through tough time or/and abuse to reach a lot of them. Publicity for mental health event which primary will be held in four regions in Tanzania as well as airtime in Tv and Radio station.
