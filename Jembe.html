The Challenge being addressed

Small scale farmers in Lakezone regions are facing the problem of market for their goods. According to the FAO report 2018, in Tanzania there are 19 million small scale farmers. 98% of these farmers have no access to formal markets. Only 2% small scale farmers are using formal markets. In the lake zone region of Mwanza, Geita, Mara, Simiyu and Kagera small scale farmers are facing the same problem. The root cause is that there is no relevant information to farmers about where they can get their market.


Who is the solution for? Describe your customer

Our solution is to provide market information to these small scale farmers using the Jembe App and Jembe Forum where farmers will know where the market is. This App will be found offline. We will connect small scale farmers with market places
