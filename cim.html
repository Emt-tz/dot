Introduction

CIM intends to offer an ever-lasting solution to issues that limits rural smallholder rice farmers in Usa River ward to access loans from formal and semi-formal credit service providers by providing loans and creating a rice farming training system that is effective and within their reach.
Our Microfinance will be located near the farming areas and will provide free collateral loans, with 7% interest, commercial rice farming techniques and market link to smallholder rice producer farmers in groups. The services will be provided on premise and through digital (CIM-Farmcare) - an app that is fast and effective for loan delivery and farming education services.

Vision

Offering everlasting solutions to issues that limit rural smallholder rice producer farmers in Usa-river to access Loans from formal and semi-formal credit service providers

Mission

Provide free collateral loans with affordable interest, which can be accessed physically or through the CIM farm care app, facilitate commercial farming techniques and create market links for smallholder rice farmers.


The Challenge being addressed

The innovation addresses the issues of poverty, unmet financial services, lack of proper farming techniques and insufficient market to smallholder rice farmers in Usa River ward. Poverty in the Usa River ward is still prevalent, 40% of families are poor and significant numbers of people are still financially excluded. Overall, the market is still grossly under-served especially for youths and women farmers living in suburban and rural areas who lack collateral to secure loans from formal banks.


Who is the solution for? Describe your customer

The CIM social innovation is intended to help 50 poor and under-served Usa-river youths and single parent mothers aged 18-35 who engage in small scale rice farming.


What support do you need to bring the innovation to life

● Technical support, including an agricultural personnel to facilitate commercial rice farming trainings and IT support to help us create the (USSD) CIM farm care application
● Seed funds to start up the social innovation
● Capacity building and consulting
