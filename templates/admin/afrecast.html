Introduction

Our social innovation idea is creating beauty out of waste and make the society knows the value of preventing the environment from waste and take the waste as opportunity for their own development by applying handcrafted skills to produce something beautiful and usable,also we will make the society to understand the value of art as art is a visual presentation in what is happening in the community so the society will have to take art as means of communication because art is always connected to the society.

Vision

Our vision in two years from now is to increase the number of people who will participate and engage in the field of art and take it as a serious career goal.

Mission

Our mission is to remove the bad perception concerning art that art is a leisure and not a career goal so we want the society to take art as a serious career goal as they take in other fields like doctor and engineers.


The Challenge being addressed

The challenge is that we are addressing is environmental pollution as it as been a big problem in the society because there is a waste all over the place and all surrounded environment and make the environment uncomfortable for the people around the society so we want to make the environment comfortable for us and for our future generations and also the project we will solve the problem of unemployment to the 7 out of 10 youth in our society by proving skills and offer the employment opportunities to the people within the community and in progress we will be reducing the rate of poverty in the society.


Who is the solution for? Describe your customer

Our customers are foreigners who are staying within my society. Tourists and local people are few from middle and upper class but we want to expand our customer base by engaging people from my community to participate and get the opportunity to practice and enjoy the art field.


What support do you need to bring the innovation to life

The support that we need is mentorship,General training, research training, business management training and funding inorder to guide and bring our ideals into life.
