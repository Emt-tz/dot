Vision

To bring to life every opportunity in the livestock keeping sector in Hanang District and promote a healthy lifestyle in the community.

Mission

Provide customers with safe and affordable dairy products in Hanang Manyara


The Challenge being addressed

Small scale cattle farmers in Hanang district find it difficult to find a reliable market for their products, they pack their products in traditional jars and plastic bottles which may expose milk to contaminations and spoilage.
