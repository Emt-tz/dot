Introduction

Clarify is a digital platform creating a safe space for people suffering with mental illnesses to anonymously share their troubles and receive emotional support conveniently. Fostering an open communication cultured community built upon support rather than judgment as we provide counselling to help communities which cannot afford expensive psychotherapy sessions.

The Challenge being addressed

According to data from WHO its estimated 800,000 people die due to suicide every year,which is one person every 40seconds. Where victims fail to seek help on time because of the heavy burden of the stigma our communities entertain around mental illnesses.


Who is the solution for? Describe your customer

Clarity works to support the youth from 18-45 who are troubled emotionally and have mental illnesses to have comfort, heal and have better lives.
