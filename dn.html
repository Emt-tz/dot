Vision

To play a great role in attaining excellent education in our country whereby every teacher has the ability to use clean and smart chalk to deliver quality education.

Mission

We work collectively to produce affordable and premium quality products that make users comfortable, happy and healthy.


The Challenge being addressed

About 80% of teachers and students in Zanzibar Stone town have been affected by coughing associated diseases caused by using dust chalk in schools. Dustless chalks take a lot of time to reach the island because they are imported from the UAE and China. The imported chalks costs are too high and many learning institutions can not afford them hence the use of harmful chalks.


Who is the solution for? Describe your customer

The solution is for educational stakeholders who own Education Institutions and chalk suppliers

  1. Private school owners: about 20-25 schools in Stone town struggle a lot to get quality chalks for their teachers
  2. Government Institutions: About 25-30 Government schools, struggle to get quality chalks for their teachers for better education
  3. Whole sellers: There are only 2 of them in Stone Town who buy chalk outside the country and supply them in different private schools
